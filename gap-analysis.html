Critical Data Gaps

PO to Installation Linkage
CRITICAL

Problem: No connection between purchased materials (PO data) and installation items (audit data). Cannot identify which PO items correspond to installation quantities.

Impact: Cannot answer "Is material for work package X received?" or "What work is delayed due to missing materials?"
Solution: Implement material tracking system with unique identifiers (Tag Numbers, Line Numbers, Equipment IDs)
Actual Installation Progress
CRITICAL

Problem: ERN_QTY (Earned Quantity) fields are empty in all discipline audit files. No actual completion data available.

Impact: Cannot calculate actual installation progress, productivity, earned value, or forecast completion dates.
Solution: Field teams must update ERN_QTY and milestone completion status weekly
Material Receipt Documentation
CRITICAL

Problem: Missing formal receiving reports with dates, inspection results, and staging locations.

Impact: Cannot track days from delivery to installation, material currently on-site, or damage during handling.
Solution: Implement receiving inspection process with QR code scanning and mobile app
Material Required Dates
HIGH

Problem: No Material Required Dates (MRD) aligned to installation schedule.

Impact: Cannot identify materials that will cause delays or prioritize expediting efforts.
Solution: Integrate with project schedule to generate MRDs for each work package
NCR/OSD Tracking
MEDIUM

Problem: NCR (Non-Conformance) and OSD (Over-Short-Damaged) fields exist but contain no data.

Impact: Cannot track quality issues, supplier problems, or material replacement needs.
Solution: Implement quality inspection workflow with issue logging
Material Location Tracking
LOW

Problem: No tracking of material location after receipt (laydown area, warehouse, staging).

Impact: Difficulty finding materials on-site, inefficient material handling.
Solution: Add location field to receiving process and track material movements

Recommended Next Steps

  1. Implement PO-to-Installation material linking system (see Material Tracking page)

  3. Create receiving inspection checklist with date/location capture
